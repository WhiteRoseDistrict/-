In April 2018, White Rose District opened its doors on Calle Rey de España, downtown Fuengirola, and became the home of Malaga skate culture. At its core was the gang of rebellious young Malaga skaters and artists who became the store’s staff, crew and customers. White Rose District grew to be the embodiment of the downtown culture, playing an integral part in its constant regeneration. Skaters, punks, hip-hop heads- the young counter culture at large- all gravitated towards White Rose District. While it grew into a downtown institution, White Rose District established itself as a brand known for its quality, style and authenticity. Over its short history, White Rose District has worked with some of our generation’s most groundbreaking designers, artists, photographers and musicians- all who have helped continue to define its unique identity and attitude.
